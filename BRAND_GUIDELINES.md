## üë• Target Audience

### Primary Audience: Students
- **UPSC aspirants**
- **Competitive exam students** (SSC, Banking, Railways, etc.)
- **Age**: 18-35 years
- **Goals**: Practice past papers, identify weak areas, improve scores
- **Pain Points**: Limited access to quality questions, lack of AI explanations, no progress tracking

### Secondary Audience: Coaching Institutes
- **Coaching centers**
- **Educational institutions**
- **Private tutors**
- **Goals**: Create mock tests, manage students, track performance
- **Pain Points**: Time-consuming question creation, lack of analytics, manual grading

## üìê Design Principles (Basecamp-Inspired)

### Layout
- **Spacious**: Generous whitespace
- **Full-width sections**: No container constraints on hero
- **Grid-based**: Clean, organized feature grids
- **Minimal navigation**: Simple, unobtrusive

### Typography
- **Large, bold headlines**: Make a statement
- **Clear hierarchy**: Easy to scan
- **Readable**: Comfortable line heights

### Visual Elements
- **Subtle animations**: Smooth, not distracting
- **Gradient backgrounds**: Modern, engaging
- **Clean icons**: Simple, recognizable
- **High contrast**: Excellent readability

### User Experience
- **Clear value proposition**: Immediately understand benefits
- **Social proof**: Statistics, testimonials
- **Easy navigation**: Smooth scrolling, clear sections
- **Mobile-first**: Responsive at all sizes

---

## üé¨ Animation Guidelines

### Micro-interactions
- **Hover effects**: Scale, shadow, color transitions
- **Button states**: Active, hover, disabled
- **Card interactions**: Lift on hover

### Page Animations
- **Fade-in**: Content appears smoothly
- **Scroll-triggered**: Elements animate on view
- **Blob animations**: Background elements (subtle)

### Performance
- **Smooth 60fps**: All animations optimized
- **Reduced motion**: Respect user preferences
- **Lazy loading**: Images and heavy content
