## üé® Design Principles Applied

### Spacing
- **Generous padding**: 20-32 (py-20 to py-32)
- **Section gaps**: Large margins between sections
- **Card padding**: Comfortable internal spacing
- **Whitespace**: Used strategically for focus

### Typography Hierarchy
1. **Hero**: 5xl-7xl, Bold
2. **Section Headings**: 4xl-5xl, Bold
3. **Card Titles**: xl, Bold
4. **Body Text**: base-lg, Regular
5. **Small Text**: sm, Regular

### Color Usage
- **Primary actions**: Blue-cyan gradient
- **Secondary actions**: White with blue border
- **Text**: Gray-900 (headings), Gray-600 (body)
- **Backgrounds**: White, Gray-50 (alternating)
- **Accents**: Plan-specific colors (green, blue, orange)

### Interactive Elements
- **Buttons**: Scale on hover, shadow effects
- **Cards**: Lift on hover, border color change
- **Links**: Color transition on hover
- **Navigation**: Backdrop blur on scroll

### Animations
- **Blob backgrounds**: Subtle, slow movement
- **Fade-in**: Content appears on load
- **Hover effects**: Scale, shadow, color
- **Testimonial carousel**: Auto-rotate with indicators

## üìù Notes

This design follows Basecamp's philosophy of:
- **Clarity over cleverness**
- **Spacious over cramped**
- **Bold over timid**
- **Simple over complex**

The page communicates value immediately, guides users naturally through the sections, and makes it easy to take action. Every element serves a purpose, and nothing is included "just because."
